SACCO Management System

1. Executive Summary

SACCO Management System is a complete, cloud‑based platform that digitises and automates all core operations of a Savings and Credit Cooperative Organisation (SACCO). Built for scalability and security, it serves everyone – from members who want 24/7 access to their accounts, to managers who need real‑time oversight, to super‑administrators who run multiple SACCOs.

Our system eliminates paperwork, reduces processing time, and brings total transparency to every transaction.

2. Who Is This For?

RolePrimary Needs

SACCO ManagersApprove loans, manage staff (accountants, credit officers), generate reports
AccountantsApprove member deposits, reconcile accounts, produce financial statements
Credit OfficersMonitor loan repayments, track overdue accounts

3. Key Features by Module

3.1 Member Portal (Self‑Service)

FeatureDescription
DashboardAt‑a‑glance balance, active loans, recent transactions
Savings AccountView balance, see pending deposits, download statement
Deposit InitiationRequest a cash deposit; funds appear after admin approval of received funds
Loan RequestApply for a loan, see eligibility (max 3x savings), track status
Loan RepaymentPay from savings balance, cash or mobile money to be added soon.
Transaction HistoryFull audit trail of deposits, withdrawals, loan payments
Profile ManagementUpdate personal details, change password, upload photo
Referral ProgramTo be added to enable member to get a unique referral code; earn commissions on referred members’ deposits

3.2 Manager & Staff Portals

Manager Dashboard

  • Overview of SACCO health (total members, savings, loans outstanding)
  • Approve or reject loan requests (with notes)
  • Create and manage staff accounts (Accountant, Credit Officer)
  • View system‑wide reports
  • Deactivate/reactivate members

Accountant Dashboard

  • Approve member deposit requests (cash only for now)
  • View all approved and pending transactions
  • Reconcile daily collections
  • Generate financial reports for key KPIs.

Credit Officer Dashboard

  • Monitor active loans
  • Track overdue loans and contact borrowers
  • View repayment history

3.3 External Loan Management

  • Register non‑member borrowers (name, contact, national ID)
  • Capture collateral details (description, estimated value)
  • Select a member guarantor (must have savings balance)
  • Admin approves/rejects; payments recorded manually
  • Full payment history with method tracking (cash, bank, mobile money)

3.4 Dual‑Account System (Staff as Members)

  • Staff can have a separate member account for personal savings/loans
  • Linked accounts prevent conflict of interest (staff cannot approve their own or linked member’s loans)
  • One‑click switching between admin and member views

3.5 Security & Audit

  • All passwords hashed (bcrypt)
  • Session management with secure cookies
  • Role‑based access control (RBAC)
  • Every action logged (who, when, IP address)

3.6 Mobile Responsive & User Friendly

  • Works on any device (phone, tablet, desktop)
  • Clean Bootstrap 5 interface with icons (ready for future integration)
